Avatars

Water

Originated from the Moon and Ocean Spirits, Waterbending is a powerful bending that is both graceful and powerful. Alternative styles include: Bloodbending and Healing.

Above: Demonstration of Waterbending and alternative styles

Earth

Originated from the Badgermoles, Earthbending is the element of substance, being tough and direct in offense, and heavelly focused on counter attacking. Alternative styles include: Lavabending, Metalbending and Sandbending.

Above: Demonstration of Earthbending and alternative styles

Fire

Originated from Dragons, Firebending stands out as a destructive bending ability that can be generated from the performer. Alternative styles include: Combustbending and Lightning.

Above: Demonstration of Firebending and alternative styles

Air

Originated from Flying Bisons, Airbending is the element of freedom, being used by Air Nomads. It relies on agility and redirection of attacks. Alternative styles include: Flight and Spiritual Projection.

Above: Demonstration of Airbending and alternative styles
